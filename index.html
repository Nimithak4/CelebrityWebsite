Introduction

Shehnaaz Gill – Shehnaaz Kaur Gill, a popular singer, model, and actress, mainly works in the Punjabi film and music industry. She is known as the Katrina of Punjab.

She is popular amongst her friends as Sana Gill and among her fans as Shehnaaz Sana.

About Me

Gill was born on 27 January 1993 or 1994 and raised in Punjab, India. She is of Punjabi descent and belongs to a Sikh family. She loved to act and sing, and dreamt to be an actor since childhood.

She began her modelling career with the 2015 music video, Shiv Di Kitaab.In 2017, she debuted as an actress in Punjabi film Sat Shri Akaal England. In 2019, she participated in the reality show Bigg Boss 13 where she finished in third place.
